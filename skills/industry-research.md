# Industry Research: Value Chain Deep Scan

Map the full investment landscape for $ARGUMENTS — from macro thesis to actionable stock picks across the value chain.

## Execution

### Step 1: Investment logic chain

Build the causal chain:

```
Underlying Trend (demographic / technological / regulatory / macro)
  → Drives: what demand does this create?
  → Creates: what is deterministic about this demand?
  → Benefits: which parts of the value chain capture value?
```

### Step 3: Full company scan — NSE/BSE + Global

For each layer, identify:
- **Listed Indian companies** (NSE/BSE)
- **Listed global companies** (with India exposure or comparable)
- **Unlisted Indian leaders** (note IPO pipeline if relevant)

Anti-bias checklist:
- [ ] Have I included small/mid-cap companies, not just large caps?
- [ ] Have I included unlisted leaders that dominate their segment?
- [ ] Have I checked for regional/vernacular players the English press misses?
- [ ] Have I checked for the "picks and shovels" angle — who supplies the winners?
